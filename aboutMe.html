About Me

Sarah Hyun. Dog mom. Yarn enthusiast.

My first piece I crocheted ended up looking like an amorphic chicken embryo. It wasn't the best looking project, but it got me started when I was around 8 years old. After countless hours of watching youtube videos and experimenting on my own, I realized my love for the craft. I am particularly interested in home decor. I am a firm believer in unlimited amount of soft blankets and pillows to create the ultimate cozy piece of heaven to call your own. The yarn projects I make are inspired by my own love for comfort, calm, and peace in the home, and of course, my dog. I'm just any other girl, working a full time job to support my furbaby. I love to explore new places, play music, eat great food, and make some cool yarn creations along the way. I live an average life in the most fulfilling way I know how.

P.S. Ollie will make an appearance in almost everything. She is life.
